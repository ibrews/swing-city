A living neon city at night. Sling a web, swing between skyscrapers, and see how much trouble you can cause before the night's over.

controls (tap to expand/collapse)

MOVE

keyboard: WASD / arrows  ·  gamepad & VR: left stick  ·  touch: left joystick

LOOK / TURN

mouse: move mouse  ·  gamepad: right stick  ·  touch: drag right half of screen  ·  VR: right stick — Y (up/down) snap-turns, X (left/right) smooth-turns

SWING (WEB)

hold LMB or SPACE — swing, let go to release  ·  tap LMB or SPACE — jump (tap again in the air to swing instead)  ·  gamepad: A/Cross or right trigger  ·  touch: tap the screen  ·  VR: either trigger or right A

WALL CLIMB

hold the swing input against a wall to crawl up it (all platforms)

SPRINT

keyboard: shift  ·  gamepad & VR: push the stick to full deflection, or right trigger  ·  touch: SPRINT button

FIRST / THIRD PERSON

keyboard: V  ·  gamepad & VR: right B

RESPAWN

keyboard: R  ·  gamepad: X/Square  ·  touch: R button  ·  VR: left Y

MENU / EXIT VR

keyboard: M — menu  ·  VR: left X — exit VR (opens menu too)

SHOW/HIDE HUD

keyboard: H  ·  gamepad: Y/Triangle  ·  VR: left stick click  ·  touch: HUD button

HOW TO WIN: there's no ending — just a high score. Smash into cars while swinging to send them flying (+25), grab the glowing orbs for points (+10), and joust or knock other players around to rack up bonus points too — a few unusual-looking orbs do something other than add points. Cross the glowing boundary wall and you'll get bounced hard back toward the city -- no falling off the edge.
