SWING CITY

A living neon city at night. Sling a web, swing between skyscrapers, and see how much trouble you can cause before the night's over.

click to enter

mouse — look  ·  hold LMB or SPACE — shoot a web and swing (let go to release)
tap LMB or SPACE — jump (in the air, tap again to fire a web instead)  ·  hold against a wall — crawl up it
WASD — run / steer in the air  ·  shift — sprint  ·  R — respawn

HOW TO WIN: there's no ending — just a high score. Smash into cars while swinging to send them flying (+25), grab the glowing orbs for points (+10), and joust or knock other players around to rack up bonus points too — a few unusual-looking orbs do something other than add points. Cross the glowing boundary wall and the screen turns red -- you've got about 2 seconds to get back inside before it's over.
